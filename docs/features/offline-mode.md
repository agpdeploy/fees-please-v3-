# Offline Mode & PWA Guide

Fees Please is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means you can install it directly to your phone's home screen like a native app. It also features a robust offline mode, ensuring you can manage Game Day even when you are at a sports field with zero cellular reception.

## 1. Installing the App on Your Phone

To get the best experience, install the app directly to your device.

### For iOS (iPhone/iPad):
1. Open the **Safari** browser on your device.
2. Navigate to `https://app.feesplease.app` and log in.
3. Tap the **Share** icon at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up).
4. Scroll down the share menu and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Confirm by tapping **Add** in the top right corner.
6. The Fees Please icon will now appear on your home screen. Tap it to launch the app without the browser UI.

### For Android:
1. Open the **Chrome** browser on your device.
2. Navigate to `https://app.feesplease.app` and log in.
3. A prompt should appear at the bottom of the screen saying **Add Fees Please to Home screen**. Tap it.
   * *If the prompt doesn't appear, tap the three-dot menu icon in the top right and select **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.*
4. Confirm the installation. The app will now be available on your home screen or app drawer.

## 2. Preparing for Offline Use

If you know you will be at a venue with poor internet connection, prepare your app beforehand:

1. While you still have a stable Wi-Fi or cellular connection, open the Fees Please app.
2. Navigate to the **Game Day** portal and open your upcoming match.
3. Loading the page automatically caches the match details, roster, and player data directly to your device.
4. You are now safe to lose connection!

## 3. Operating Offline on Game Day

When your device loses connection, the app will automatically switch to Offline Mode.

1. You will see an **Offline Indicator** (typically a cloud with a slash or an orange warning banner) at the top of your screen indicating you have lost connection.
2. Continue using the app as normal:
   * Add players to the lineup.
   * Toggle payment statuses (Cash, Card, PayID).
   * Enter the Umpire Return fee.
3. **Important:** Do NOT close the app completely or log out while offline. You can minimize the app, but force-closing it may result in lost data before it has a chance to sync.

## 4. Syncing Data Back to the Cloud

Once your game is finished and you return to an area with an internet connection:

1. Open the Fees Please app.
2. The app will detect the internet connection and automatically begin syncing your offline changes to the main database.
3. Watch the Offline Indicator at the top of the screen; it will change to a **Syncing...** status.
4. Once the sync is complete, the indicator will disappear or display a green **Synced** checkmark.
5. You can now safely finalize the match and close the app.
