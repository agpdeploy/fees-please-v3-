# dAIve AI Assistant

dAIve is your integrated AI assistant, built directly into the Fees Please platform. It helps administrators answer questions, query database statistics, and automate repetitive tasks using natural language.

## 1. Accessing dAIve

1. Log in to your Fees Please account.
2. Locate the **dAIve Chat Widget** in the bottom right corner of your screen (usually represented by a chat bubble or robot icon).
3. Click the widget to expand the chat interface.

## 2. Asking Questions and Querying Data

You can ask dAIve questions about your account's data just like you would ask a human assistant. 

1. In the chat interface, click on the text input box at the bottom.
2. Type your question in plain English. For example:
   * *"Who owes the most money for this season?"*
   * *"What was our total revenue for the month of May?"*
   * *"Which player has attended the most matches?"*
3. Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button.
4. dAIve will securely query your ledger and roster database, crunch the numbers, and provide a direct answer within the chat window.

## 3. Using Pre-Built Prompts

If you aren't sure what to ask, dAIve offers helpful suggestions.

1. Open the dAIve chat interface.
2. Above the text input box, you will see a series of **Quick Prompts** (small, clickable bubbles with suggested questions).
3. Click on any of these prompts (e.g., *"Show me unpaid fees"*).
4. dAIve will immediately execute the query and return the results without you needing to type anything.

## 4. Closing and Minimizing

1. Once you have the information you need, you can click the **Minimize** button (often a downward arrow or an 'X') at the top right of the chat window.
2. Your conversation history is temporarily saved during your session, so you can reopen the widget later to review the answers.
