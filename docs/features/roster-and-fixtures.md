# Roster & Fixture Management

Keeping your team's roster and schedule up to date is crucial for smooth game day operations. This guide walks you through manually managing players and setting up your season fixtures.

## 1. Adding Players to the Roster

To add new full-time or casual players to your master database:

1. Log in and navigate to the **Roster** tab in the left navigation sidebar.
2. Click the **+ Add Player** button at the top right of the screen.
3. A modal window will appear. Fill in the player's details:
   * **First & Last Name**
   * **Email Address** (Optional, but recommended for automated reminders)
   * **Phone Number** (Optional)
   * **Player Status:** Choose whether they are a "Full-time" squad member or a "Casual" fill-in.
4. Click **Save Player**. They will now appear in your master roster list and be available for selection on Game Day.

## 2. Managing the Master Roster

From the Roster page, you can easily manage player information and track their overall financials.

1. **Viewing Details:** Click on any player's name in the list to open their profile. Here you can see their match history, total fees paid, and outstanding balances.
2. **Editing Details:** On their profile, click the **Edit** icon (pencil) to update their contact info or status.
3. **Removing Players:** If a player leaves the club, click the **Archive** or **Delete** button on their profile. Archiving preserves their historical ledger data, which is recommended over full deletion.

## 3. Manually Creating Fixtures

If you are not using the automated PlayHQ integration, you must manually create your match schedule.

1. Navigate to the **Fixtures** tab using the left navigation sidebar.
2. Click the **+ Add Fixture** button in the top right corner.
3. Fill out the match details in the form:
   * **Opponent Name:** Who are you playing against?
   * **Date & Time:** Use the date picker to set the kickoff time.
   * **Venue/Court:** Enter the location (e.g., "Court 2, Main Stadium").
   * **Match Type:** Select if it is a Regular Season, Final, or Friendly match.
4. Click **Save Fixture**.
5. The match will now appear in your upcoming schedule and will be accessible via the Game Day portal when the date arrives.

## 4. Editing or Deleting a Fixture

If a match is rescheduled or cancelled:

1. On the **Fixtures** page, locate the match in the list.
2. Click the **three dots (options menu)** next to the match.
3. Select **Edit** to change the date, time, or venue. Click Save when done.
4. Select **Delete** if the match has been permanently cancelled. 
   * *Note: You cannot delete a match that has already been Finalized in the Game Day portal, as it contains locked financial data.*
