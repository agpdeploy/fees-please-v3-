# Ledger & Accounting

The Ledger provides a complete financial overview, tracking every dollar billed and collected across your entire account.

## 1. Billing vs. Collections

- **Fees Billed**: Match fees are generated and charged automatically when a fixture is finalized or a player is added to the active roster.
- **Payments Collected**: Logs payments received via Square, Cash, or PayID.
- **Individual Player Balances**: Every player has an individual ledger statement showing their balance status (Paid, Outstanding, or In Credit).

---

## 2. Admin Ledger Views

- **Roster Overview**: Account Admins can view a list of all players and their current balances.
- **Cross-Team Ledger**: Consolidated summary screens allow Account Admins to view total billed, collected, and outstanding balances across all teams within the account, rather than switching between teams one by one.
- **Manual Transactions**: Admins can log manual adjustments, charges, or write-offs for any player.

---

## 3. Account Credits & Debt Consumption

- **Apply Credit**: If a player prepays or is owed money, admins can apply account credits directly to their balance.
- **Chronological Debt Consumption**: When a player has multiple outstanding debts (e.g., unpaid fees from week 1, week 2, etc.), the system automatically applies any new payments to their oldest outstanding invoices first.
- **Prepayments**: Payments exceeding the current outstanding amount are automatically stored as positive credits and applied to the next match fee.
