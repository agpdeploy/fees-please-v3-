# Ledger & Accounting

The Ledger & Accounting module is your source of truth for all financial transactions. It automatically tracks game day fees, umpire payments, and manual adjustments, allowing you to reconcile your team's bank account with ease.

## 1. Accessing the Ledger

1. Log in to your Fees Please account.
2. In the left navigation sidebar, click on **Ledger**.
3. By default, the Ledger will display the current active season's financial summary.

## 2. Navigating the Ledger Dashboard

When you open the Ledger, you will see several key components:

1. **High-Level Summary Widgets:** 
   * At the top of the page, view the total **Revenue Collected**, total **Umpire Fees Paid**, and your **Net Balance**.
2. **Transaction Table:**
   * Below the summary, you will find a detailed table of every transaction.
   * Each row displays the **Date**, **Player Name**, **Match Name**, **Payment Method** (e.g., Square, Cash, PayID), and the **Amount**.
3. **Filtering & Sorting:**
   * Click the **Filter** button above the table to narrow down transactions.
   * You can filter by **Date Range**, **Player**, or **Payment Method** (useful when reconciling your cash box versus your bank statement).
   * Click on any column header (like "Date" or "Amount") to sort the data ascending or descending.

## 3. Creating Manual Adjustments

Sometimes you need to log a transaction that didn't occur on Game Day (e.g., buying new equipment, a player paying fees in advance).

1. On the Ledger page, click the **+ Add Transaction** button in the top right corner.
2. A modal window will appear. Fill out the following fields:
   * **Transaction Type:** Select either **Income** or **Expense**.
   * **Amount:** Enter the dollar amount.
   * **Description:** Write a brief note about the transaction (e.g., "Purchased new training bibs").
   * **Date:** Select the date the transaction occurred.
   * **Player (Optional):** If this transaction is tied to a specific player (like a season up-front payment), select their name from the dropdown.
3. Click **Save Transaction**. The ledger will instantly update your Net Balance.

## 4. Reconciling with Your Bank

To ensure your team's bank account matches Fees Please:

1. Open your actual bank account statement.
2. On the Fees Please Ledger page, apply a filter for **Card** and **PayID** (excluding Cash, since cash isn't in your bank until deposited).
3. Compare the total shown in the Fees Please ledger to the deposits in your bank statement.
4. If everything aligns, your accounts are reconciled!

## 5. Exporting Financial Data

If you need to share financials with your club committee or an accountant:

1. Navigate to the Ledger page.
2. Apply any desired filters (e.g., select the entire "Winter 2026" season).
3. Click the **Export** button located at the top right of the transaction table.
4. Select **Download CSV**.
5. The file will download to your device, ready to be opened in Excel or Google Sheets.
