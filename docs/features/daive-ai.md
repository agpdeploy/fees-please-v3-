# dAIve AI Assistant

**dAIve** is your AI-powered copilot inside Fees Please. Built directly into the chat portal, dAIve helps you manage your account settings, extract data, and get quick financial insights.

## 1. Interaction Capabilities

You can chat with dAIve in natural language to perform multiple queries without digging through tabs:
- **Query Ledger Breakdown**: Ask *"Show me who hasn't paid yet"* or *"What is Lachy's balance?"*.
- **Retrieve Fixtures**: Ask *"When is our next match?"* or *"Who do we play next week?"*.
- **Financial Summary**: Ask *"What are our total collections for this season?"*.

---

## 2. Dynamic Documentation Search

If you are stuck on how to configure settings:
- Ask dAIve *"How do I connect Square?"* or *"How do I sync from PlayHQ?"*.
- dAIve will query the Confluence space using CQL search terms, find the relevant user guides, and summarize the step-by-step instructions for you directly in the chat window.

---

## 3. Onboarding & Upload Copilot

During roster and fixture setup:
- dAIve acts as the background extraction engine, handling PDF and image parsing.
- It provides step-by-step guidance and status updates, including cyclic loading logs to show you what stage the processing is at.
