# Referral Program Guide

The Fees Please Referral Program allows you to invite other teams and accounts to the platform and track your successful invites. When your referrals become active or upgrade to a paid plan, your account gets rewarded!

## 1. Accessing Your Referral Link

To start inviting other teams, you need to grab your unique referral link:

1. **Log in** to your Fees Please account and navigate to the main **Dashboard**.
2. On the main navigation sidebar (or the top tab menu), click on **Referrals**.
3. At the top of the Referrals page, you will see a text box containing your unique referral link (e.g., `https://app.feesplease.app/auth/register?ref=YOUR_CODE`).
4. Click the **Copy** button next to the link.
5. Share this link with friends, team managers, or club administrators. When they click the link, they will be taken to the registration page with your referral code automatically applied.

## 2. Tracking Your Referrals

Once people sign up using your link, you can track their status directly from the **Referrals** page:

1. Scroll down to the **Referral Stats** section on the Referrals page.
2. Here, you will see two main counters:
   * **Pending Referrals:** The number of accounts that have signed up but haven't yet met the criteria for completion.
   * **Completed Referrals:** The number of accounts that have successfully converted (see completion criteria below).
3. Below the stats, the **Recent Referrals** table lists each account that signed up using your link along with their current status.

## 3. Referral Completion Criteria

A referral is marked as **Completed** (or successful) when the referred account achieves either of the following:

* **Converts to a Paid Plan:** The referred account upgrades from a Free plan to a Paid subscription plan.
* **Completes Required Activity on a Free Plan:** The referred account remains on the Free plan but completes the appropriate amount of games or transactions.

Once a referral meets these criteria, it contributes toward your account's rewards. Keep sharing your link to help your teams and earn more!
